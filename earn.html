Meet LBRY.TV

For most users, LBRY will be a place where they can find great videos, music, ebooks, and more: imagine a vast digital library that is available on all of your devices. But under the hood, LBRY is many components working together.
LBRY.TV is a YouTube alternative, where you earn LBRY tokens, you'll later on exchange to BitCoins.

How do I earn tokens?

First and foremost, you earn tokens by having making an account here LBRY.TV, then going to their rewards page and completing various tasks under rewards section and inviting friends via invites section.

Get lot of LBRY for no work whatsoever

Where do can I exchange LBRY to BTC?

Head over to Bittrex and get your LBRY tokens exchanged to BTC. At that point, you literally have cash in your hands and you'll be getting more on a monthly basis! That's it, enjoy!
